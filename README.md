# 📊 Employee Attrition Analysis

Tools: MySQL | Power BI | Python

End-to-end HR analytics project analyzing 4,382 employees across 4 datasets to identify why employees leave, predict who is at risk next, and quantify the financial cost — **₹261.48M lost to attrition**.

## Tools
**MySQL** — 7 analytical views | **Power BI** — 4-page interactive dashboard | **Python** — Random Forest model, 1–10 risk scoring, automated PDF report

## Key Findings
- 🔴 HR department attrition at **29.41%** — nearly 2x company average of 16.09%
- 🔴 R&D bleeding most financially — **₹172M** due to high average salaries
- 🔴 Frequent travellers leaving at highest rate across **all salary bands**
- 🟡 Overworked employees leave at **29%** vs 11% underworked — workload is a major driver
- 🟡 **256 current employees** flagged High or Critical risk by ML model

## ML Model
Random Forest | Accuracy: **78.45%** | AUC: **0.83** | Risk scores assigned to all 3,677 current employees
